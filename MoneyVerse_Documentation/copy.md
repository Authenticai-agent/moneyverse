# MoneyVerse Copy System

All user-facing strings should use stable localization keys. Do not hard-code critical copy in feature components.

## Voice

- Encouraging
- Respectful
- Plain-language
- Non-judgmental
- Age-appropriate
- Transparent about uncertainty and simulation

## Prohibited language

Never use:

- “You failed.”
- “Bad money choice.”
- “You are irresponsible.”
- “Only losers spend that way.”
- “Your pet is sick because you spent money.”
- “Guaranteed return.”
- “Risk-free investment.”
- “Ask your parents for their card.”
- “Invite more friends or lose your streak.”

## Authentication

`auth.login.title`  
Welcome back

`auth.login.invalid_credentials`  
We couldn’t sign you in with those details.

`auth.rate_limited`  
Too many attempts were made. Please try again later.

Do not reveal whether a specific email exists.

## Child learning

`lesson.retry`  
That choice had a tradeoff. Try another strategy.

`lesson.complete`  
Nice work. You practiced a new money skill.

`scenario.unexpected_expense`  
Unexpected expenses happen. Let’s adjust the plan.

## Parent controls

`parent.child_created`  
The child profile is ready.

`parent.consent_required`  
Review and approve how this feature uses your child’s information.

## Security

`security.session_revoked`  
This session was signed out to help protect your account.

`security.sensitive_input_warning`  
Please remove banking passwords, card numbers, Social Security numbers, or other sensitive information.
