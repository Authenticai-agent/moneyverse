# Consent

## Consent records

Store policy version, scope, actor, subject, timestamp, method, and withdrawal state.

## Separate consent scopes

- Core account use
- Child profile creation
- Classroom participation
- Optional analytics
- External sharing
- Future AI processing
- Future Plaid connection
- Future marketing communications

Consent for one scope does not imply consent for another.

## Reconsent

Require reconsent after material changes to data use, external providers, or child-facing functionality when legally or ethically appropriate.

## Withdrawal

Withdrawal must stop future processing, revoke relevant access, disable integrations, and begin deletion or minimization workflows subject to documented legal obligations.
