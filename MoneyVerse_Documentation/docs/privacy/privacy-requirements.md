# Privacy Requirements

## Data minimization

Collect only information needed for authentication, family relationships, age-appropriate content, learning progress, and requested features.

Avoid:

- Full birth date
- Precise location
- Legal child name
- Child email
- Banking credentials
- Social Security number
- Full card number
- Family salary
- Unrestricted child free text

## Rights and controls

Support access, correction, export, consent withdrawal, profile deletion, account deletion, and integration disconnection.

## Advertising

No behavioral or targeted advertising to children. Do not create external ad profiles.

## Analytics

Use first-party, aggregate, minimized analytics when enabled. Exclude names, emails, exact ages, free-text responses, bank balances, and merchant descriptions.

## Compliance

Prepare for qualified review of COPPA, FERPA, state privacy laws, GDPR/UK GDPR where applicable, age-appropriate design requirements, and financial-data rules if integrations are enabled.

Do not claim compliance based solely on documentation or code.
