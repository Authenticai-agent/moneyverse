# Content Safety

## Content lifecycle

Draft → financial education review → child-safety review → accessibility review → approval → publication → periodic review → retirement.

## Required metadata

Every lesson or scenario includes:

- Age bands
- Learning objectives
- Source or reviewer
- Version
- Safety-review state
- Accessibility-review state
- Effective date for time-sensitive facts
- Next review date

## Prohibited content

- Personalized investment recommendations
- Guaranteed outcomes
- Instructions to evade guardians
- Harmful deprivation as a savings strategy
- Deceptive sales tactics
- Real-money gambling
- Instructions to obtain or misuse financial credentials
- Public comparison of family income or wealth
- Unreviewed political, medical, or legal persuasion

## Date-sensitive content

Tax rates, contribution limits, legal rules, and benefit thresholds must include an effective date and scheduled review. Prefer evergreen concepts in core gameplay.

## Generated content

Future AI-generated content remains unpublished until schema validation, safety checks, and required human review pass.
