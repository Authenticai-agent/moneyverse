# Curriculum System

## Structure

Course → module → lesson → slide/activity → assessment → mastery record.

## Age bands

- 6–8: needs, wants, coins, saving, waiting, giving
- 9–12: allowance, unit price, simple budgeting, opportunity cost, scams
- 13–15: paychecks, taxes, credit, subscriptions, entrepreneurship
- 16–18: jobs, renting, loans, insurance, emergency funds, retirement

## Mastery

A learner demonstrates mastery through multiple forms of evidence, not a single quiz score. Use retries, spaced repetition, and scenario transfer.

## Adaptation

Difficulty may change based on demonstrated performance, but age restrictions and content-safety policies remain server-enforced.

## Versioning

Progress references the lesson version attempted. Content changes must not silently rewrite historical outcomes.

## Initial domains

Needs and wants; saving; budgeting; unit price; interest; inflation; taxes; credit; loans; insurance; scams; entrepreneurship; jobs; renting; transportation; college; emergency funds; retirement; consumer rights.
