# Product Definition

## Vision

MoneyVerse is a privacy-first financial education game that helps children practice money decisions through short lessons, simulations, family missions, and a persistent virtual world.

## Primary users

- Children ages 6–18
- Parents and guardians
- Teachers
- School administrators

## Core jobs

Children learn how to budget, save, compare prices, recognize scams, understand compound growth, and practice entrepreneurship without handling real financial credentials.

Parents gain a safe way to guide financial learning without linking a bank.

Teachers gain standards-friendly assignments and private progress reporting.

## Product loops

1. Learn a concept.
2. Apply it in a scenario.
3. Receive deterministic feedback.
4. Earn non-cash learning rewards.
5. Improve a persistent virtual world.
6. Review progress with a trusted adult.

## Anti-goals

MoneyVerse is not:

- A bank
- A brokerage
- A credit-recommendation service
- A public social network
- A gambling product
- A substitute for professional financial, legal, tax, or investment advice
- A platform for targeted child advertising

## Success metrics

Prefer:

- Lesson mastery
- Repeat learning
- Parent-child discussion
- Scam-recognition improvement
- Budgeting accuracy
- Accessibility success
- Safe completion rate

Do not optimize primarily for time spent, compulsive return, public popularity, or child purchases.
